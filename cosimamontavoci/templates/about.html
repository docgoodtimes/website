About

Born in Venice, Italy. Graduated in 2015 in Fine Arts at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie.

My atelier is at the NDSM Werf, Amsterdam Noord.

I am currently working at Officina where I produce and sell my glass creations.

Officina is located in Hazenstraat 70, Amsterdam, at the corner with Elandsgracht.

Experience

I was responsible for the Greek Pavillion  at the Architecture Biennale in Venice in 2008 (exhibition: Athens by Sound) and for the Art Biennale (artist: Lucas Samaras) in 2009.

I worked with glass in Murano from 2005 to 2008. I created glass beads with: Davide Penso, Muranero (Mouaye Niang), Anfora and Centro Culturale Spiazzi.

Exhibitions

2015 - Tomb Sculpture at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie Graduation Show, Amsterdam.

2015 - Andata e Ritorno 2.0 at Ex Herion, Venice.

2014 - Vanitas at the Oude Kerk, Amsterdam.

2013 - The Fall of the Knight at the Vishal, Haarlem.
